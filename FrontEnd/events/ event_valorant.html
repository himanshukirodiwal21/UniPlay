University Valorant Invitational

Assemble your squad. Aim for glory. The digital battlefield awaits.

About The Tournament

Welcome to the premier E-Sports event on campus! The University Valorant Invitational is where strategy, skill, and teamwork collide. We're calling all student gamers to form their teams and compete for the title of university champion.

This tournament is organized to foster the growing E-Sports community within our university and provide a competitive platform for talented players. All matches will be streamed live with commentary!

Tournament Format

  • Team Size: 5 main players + 1 optional substitute.
  • Phase 1: Online Qualifiers. Single-elimination bracket to determine the top 8 teams.
  • Phase 2: Group Stage. The top 8 teams will be split into two groups, playing in a round-robin format.
  • Phase 3: Grand Finals. The top 2 teams from each group will advance to a final single-elimination bracket, to be held live on campus.

Prizes

Winning Team: The UniPlay E-Sports Trophy + ₹15,000 Cash Prize + Pro-Gaming Headsets for each player.

Runner-up Team: ₹7,500 Cash Prize + Gaming Mice for each player.

MVP Award: A special prize for the tournament's most valuable player.

Event Details

  • Qualifiers: 1st Nov 2025
  • Platform: Online / Discord
  • Game: Valorant
  • Registration Deadline: 28th Oct 2025
