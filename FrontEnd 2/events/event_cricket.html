Inter-Departmental Cricket Championship

The biggest cricketing battle on campus is back!

About The Tournament

Get ready for the most anticipated sports event of the year! The Inter-Departmental Cricket Championship brings together teams from every corner of the university to compete for the ultimate bragging rights and the coveted UniPlay Trophy. Expect thrilling matches, incredible talent, and an electrifying atmosphere.

Whether you're a seasoned player or a passionate supporter, this is an event you don't want to miss. Assemble your best squad and prepare to make your department proud!

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must consist of 11 players with up to 4 substitutes.
  • All players must be current students of the same department.
  • Matches will be played in a 10-over format during the league stage.
  • Semi-finals and Finals will be 15 overs per side.
  • Standard T20 rules apply. Umpire's decision will be final.
  • All teams must report to the ground 30 minutes before their scheduled match time.

Prizes

Winner: The UniPlay Championship Trophy + ₹10,000 Cash Prize + Gold Medals

Runner-up: ₹5,000 Cash Prize + Silver Medals

Individual awards for Man of the Series, Best Batsman, and Best Bowler.

Event Details

  • Starts: 25th Oct 2025
  • Venue: University Main Ground, Kota
  • Format: 10-Over League
  • Registration Deadline: 20th Oct 2025
