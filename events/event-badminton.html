Annual Badminton Open

Experience the ultimate test of speed, agility, and precision.

About The Tournament

The Annual Badminton Open is a highlight of the university's sports calendar, attracting top talent from all departments. This tournament celebrates the fast-paced action of badminton and promotes sportsmanship among students. Players can compete in various categories to showcase their skills.

Event Categories

  • Men's Singles
  • Women's Singles
  • Men's Doubles
  • Mixed Doubles

Rules & Regulations

  • The tournament will follow the official BWF (Badminton World Federation) rules.
  • All players must bring their own badminton rackets.
  • High-quality feather shuttles will be provided for all matches.
  • Only non-marking sports shoes are permitted on the court.
  • Players must report 20 minutes prior to their scheduled match time.

Event Details

  • Dates: 5th - 7th Nov 2025
  • Venue: Indoor Sports Complex, Kota
  • Categories: Singles & Doubles
  • Registration Deadline: 1st Nov 2025
